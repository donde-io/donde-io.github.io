FAQ

How much does Dónde cost?

Dónde’s store locator and analytics service is absolutely free. We also offer data management services, that you can check out on our pricing page.

Why is Dónde’s locator free?

Our mission is to make it easy for people to find the things they want. Whether you are looking for a new craft beer, a handbag you saw in a magazine or trying to track down that particular flavor of soup your kids like, it shouldn't take hours of your time. Giving away the locator itself helps us achieve our mission faster and focuses our efforts on helping our customers influence shoppers mobile path to purchase.

Why does it matter that Dónde is mobile first?

Because people are using their mobile phones to find your store! A clear, simple, easy to read map with directions to the nearest location/product (on mobile) can be the difference between a search and a sale.

What is a locator?

A locator is an interactive map that shows someone searching for your product/service/brand exactly where they can buy. We take the current location of the whoever is viewing the map and show them the closest place to buy your product.

Does it work on my website?

You bet! Dónde uses a universal code which you can easily paste into any site. We also provide easy integration with WordPress and Facebook. For any specific questions, please reach out - support@donde.io.

Who else uses a Dónde locator?

Dónde works with some of the largest brands in the world, as well as agencies, local breweries, wineries, and makers of all kinds.

Can I schedule a demo before I decide to install?

We thought you’d never ask! Email demo@donde.io and we'll get back to you pronto.
