Pricing Questions

When will I be charged?

You will be charged immediately upon signing up, and then every month after that.

How do I cancel?

You can cancel at any time in the billing section of the dashboard or by contacting us. There are no obligations or contracts.

How can I pay?

All major credit cards are accepted: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover.

How secure is my payment info?

All payments to Dónde are processed securely by industry leader, Stripe. Your card information never touches our servers.
