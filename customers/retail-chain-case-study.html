Challenge

Graeter’s Ice Cream, a national gourmet ice cream manufacturer, wanted  to increase the number of store visits, but were challenged with:

Increasing year over year same store revenue while driving traffic to new store openings

Objective

Target consumers through mobile local search
Drive an increase in store visits from digital

Solution

Donde used mobile-specific local landing  pages to reach consumers by:

Mobile Optimized Local Landing Pages for each retail store location
Location specific content and SEO best practices to improve organic search results

Results

With Dónde, Graeter's was able to increase digitally influenced store foot traffic by 1754%. Local landing pages also positively influenced the below results:

Same store sales are up 5.12% and check average is up 2.37%
42,840 incremental store visits were driven to stores
Per-store ROI $70.62
Newly opened Chicago store is the #1 Google result for Graeter's most important keyword in local search
