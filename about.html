About Us

Donde was founded by Anthony Nicalo and Fabien Allanic in June 2013. Investors include Mercury Fund, Chicago Ventures, Tim Kopp, Full Stack and Vine Street Ventures.

Donde is a mobile store locator service with tools to drive retail sales. 30% of website traffic is coming from mobile devices. Just making all of your desktop and tablet content fit the screen doesn't work because mobile users are almost exclusively looking for store locations. Although m-commerce is $25BB per year in the US, shoppers use the store locator 63 times for every 1 m-commerce order. In fact, 82% of mobile shoppers actually make their purchases in a physical location.

A poor mobile locator experience is costing you sales. People can't find your stuff.Get a best-in-class mobile first locator today. For free. Unlimited locations, unlimited traffic, analytics included. At Donde, we believe location is the tip of the iceberg in improving online to offline conversion.
